🔧 How It Works

🔀 Direct Service Routing

App bypasses gateway and routes directly to individual Render services:

  • /analyze/text → text-service-glgj.onrender.com
  • /detect-fake-news → fakenews-service.onrender.com
  • /analyze/image, /detect-ai-image → image-lchq.onrender.com
  • /blockchain/*, /version/* → utility-service-m2n3.onrender.com

⏱️ Timeout on first run?

Render spins down inactive services. First request takes 20-60 seconds. Be patient! ⏳

❌ Still timing out after 1min?

Check Render Dashboard - service may be down or out of credits.

💡 Hint: Try "Run Full Diagnostic" twice - first run wakes services, second run should succeed.
